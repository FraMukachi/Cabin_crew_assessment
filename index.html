Q1 of 10 Situational Judgement
SCENARIO
A passenger in Economy calls you over — their seat recline is broken. The flight is full, no spare seats. They are visibly frustrated and raising their voice.
What is your BEST course of action?
A
Tell the passenger there are no spare seats and return to your duties.
B
Apologise sincerely, acknowledge their frustration, confirm the issue, offer comfort alternatives, and inform your senior crew member.
C
Ask the passenger to lower their voice before you help them.
D
Move another passenger so this person can take their seat.
WHY THIS ANSWER
B is correct. Acknowledge first — empathy before solutions. Always inform your senior crew. Offering small alternatives shows initiative. Never tell someone to lower their voice — that escalates conflict.

SCENARIO

What is your priority action?
A
Give them water and tell them to rest.
B
Immediately assess symptoms, notify senior crew and flight deck, check for a medical professional onboard, and access the first aid kit.
C

Assessment Tactics

⏱️
Time is tight: 10 questions in 10 minutes in the SJT — 60 seconds each. Go with your first instinct. Practise until answering feels automatic.
🔒
Fullscreen is mandatory: Exit once = warning. Exit twice = automatic disqualification. Use a laptop, not mobile, for the real test.
🛡️
Safety always first: When torn between answers, pick whichever prioritises safety. Riyadh Air always prefers the safety-conscious answer.
👔
Dress smart for video: You are on camera for speaking. Smart attire, neat hair, clean background. First impressions count even online.
🎙️
Speak slowly and clearly: Clarity beats speed. Slow down, pause between ideas. You need to be understood — not fast.
🌟
Show Riyadh Air values: Weave in Hafawa (Saudi warmth) and Vision 2030. Show warmth through specific examples, not just words.
🔄
Use STAR always: Situation → Task → Action → Result. Keep S and T short. Detail goes in A. Always end with a positive R.
📵
Zero distractions: Phone off. Notifications silenced. Quiet room. Stable WiFi. Close all other tabs. Treat it like a real exam.
